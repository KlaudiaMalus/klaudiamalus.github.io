My classes

The most important thing for me in teaching is an easy and relaxed atmosphere. I believe that learning a foreign language should be pleasant and valuable, which contributes to the achievement of good results. I often highlight that all people are different. Therefore I approach each student individually and try to adapt my methodology to the student’s predispositions. I have a lot of materials and linguistic games that I use in my classes, depending on the student’s temperament, skills, and level of language proficiency.
