Translations

I offer translations from Polish-Russian and Russian-Polish. I have experience in translating contracts and legal documents for small companies. I also worked with the Office for Foreigners in Warsaw where I translated educational materials for refugees. Additional, I specialize in creative translation of advertisements, i.e., transcreation, which consists in presenting a given message in a foreign language in such a way as to evoke in new addressees the same emotions as in the case of the original message.
